Projects

Virtual Salem City

Description

The aim of this project is to create the Salem city virtually, so that everyone can explore the city by using computing devices like smart phones, tablets, laptops and personal computers. Implemented a user-friendly interface that enables easy navigation and exploration of the virtual city, providing options to switch player to vehicle or vehicle to player. This game will also guide the people who are not familiar to Salem city. An utility for economically challenged ones to fulfill their dreams to visit places to start with the Salem city.

In this game, I have build the 3D model of our Salem city. A provision for the tourists to explore Salem city before their actual visit and be familiar with or to decide the places the they wanted to visit. I made the virtual Salem city accessible to everyone, regardless of their physical or financial limitations, providing an inclusive and equitable way for people to experience the city's beauty, history, and culture.
